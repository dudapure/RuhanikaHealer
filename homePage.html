About Reiki

What is Reiki?

Reiki is a gentle, Japanese technique for stress reduction and relaxation that promotes healing. It's a non-invasive, hands-on (or distant) energy healing practice where a practitioner channels life force energy to the client.

How It Works

The practitioner acts as a conduit for Universal Life Energy, which flows through them to the client. This energy helps balance and cleanse your body's energy centers (chakras), removing blockages and supporting your natural ability to heal.

Key Benefits

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Promotes deep relaxation
  • Enhances mental clarity
  • Supports emotional healing
  • Improves sleep patterns

Reiki is a complementary therapy and is not a substitute for medical treatment. It supports your body's innate healing processes.
